Fangames (a.k.a. "Guy games" or "I Wanna" games) are a niche genre of freeware platformer games. Based on tight physics and instant respawns at their core, fangames have subgenres like action-adventure, intricate platforming, bullet hell, medleys of other fangames, and more.

Our community plays and chats about fangames 24 hours a day on the Twitch IWBTG directory. We also speedrun fangames. And the main website which indexes most of the 5000+ fangames is Delicious Fruit.

How can I get connected with the community?

A big part of our community is Twitch streams and chats. Follow the Twitch IWBTG directory and Twitch team and interact in 24/7 fangame streams. You can also register on our forums to discuss and ask questions.

Which fangames should I play?

1. Check out curated lists by Geezer, Sunbla & Zero, Paragus, Denferok.
2. Browse Delicious Fruit and search by rating, difficulty, and tags.
3. Ask friends in the community for recommendations.

What is making fangames like?

The other side of the community is making fangames! It's open to everyone, and can be very fun and rewarding. There's lots of potential for new design ideas, there are many veteran fangame creators to get help from and collaborate with, and your game is guaranteed playtime on Twitch!

If you want to jump in, check out Klazen's Tutorial. Don't hesitate to ask the community for help!

I want to know more about fangames!

All fangames are free to download and play! They're also not GPU-intensive, so older computers can run them just fine. The default controls are, by tradition, Shift to jump, Z to shoot, R to respawn, and Escape to quit.

A little backstory: In 2007, Kayin made the original I Wanna Be The Guy, which was full of humor and traps. At first, fangames of IWBTG closely followed its formula, but over the years their design evolved far beyond it. Today, fangames are an ecosystem of their own, with their own tropes and genres, and with authors and players from around the globe.

Fangames gradually became popular on Twitch simply because they're entertaining to watch. Speedrunning in particular is popular, with games like Boshy, Kamilia 2, NANG (featured in AGDQ 2017!), and many more. As a community we also host an annual weekend-long Speedrun Marathon on Twitch.
