Intro to Fangames


Fangames, also known as Guy Games and I Wanna games, are a niche subgenre of platformers. You play as an 11 by 21px kid with a double jump and tight controls, and you navigate interesting and challenging platforming. We, the community who make, play, and speedrun these games, are active 24 hours a day on Twitch, especially our Twitch Team.

Fangame difficulty can range from easy, such as I wanna be the Animus, to notoriously difficult such as I wanna Kill the Kamilia 3. But challenge isn't the only thing fangames have to offer; there's huge variety in the 5000+ fangames listed on Delicious Fruit.

Making fangames

Most fangames are made in a piece of software called GameMaker, which is powerful and beginner-friendly software for 2D game development. You're by no means forced to use GameMaker, but just be aware that the standard player physics people are used to are implemented in GameMaker.

Klazen's Tutorial is pretty much the go-to resource for starting out.

You can post in the forums if you have any programming, design, or other questions. We have quite a few experienced people who enjoy helping others out with making fangames.
