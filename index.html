Fangames Introduction

Fangames (a.k.a. "Guy games" or "I Wanna" games) are a niche genre of freeware platformers. Based on tight physics and instant respawns at their core, fangames have evolved far past the original IWBTG into their own phenomenon with subgenres and a passionate, growing community.

Our community plays and chats about fangames 24 hours a day on the Twitch IWBTG directory. We also speedrun fangames. And the main website which indexes most of the 5000+ fangames is Delicious Fruit.

How can I get connected with the community?

  • Follow the Twitch IWBTG directory and enjoy 24/7 fangame streams! Also check out our Twitch team!
  • Join the Fangame Newcomers Discord! Also join Discords of your favorite fangame Twitch streamers!
  • Register and discuss on our forums! They're a little slow but definitely active.
  • Browse Delicious Fruit and review fangames you've played!

What is making fangames like?

The other side of the community is making fangames! It's open to everyone, and can be very fun and rewarding. There's lots of potential for new design ideas, there are many talented fangame creators to give help and collaborate with, and your game is guaranteed playtime on Twitch!

If you want to jump in, check out Klazen's Tutorial. We're also happy to help on the community discord and the forums.

I want to know more about fangames!

This all started in 2007 when Kayin made the original I Wanna Be The Guy, which was full of references, traps, and humor. In the years since then, people made fangames that riffed on IWBTG's ideas and improved the player physics. Gradually fangames evolved into a phenomemon that's seperate from the original, with their own subgeneres, tropes, and inside jokes.
